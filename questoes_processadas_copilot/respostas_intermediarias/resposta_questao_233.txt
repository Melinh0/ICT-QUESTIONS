ENUNCIADO:  
26. Which of the following are the functions of the "systemctl" command?

ITENS:  
A) Collecting serial port information of the OS  
B) Viewing and controlling system services  
C) Starting, restarting, and stopping services  
D) Restarting and shutting down the system

ITEM CORRETO: B, C, D

EXPLICAÇÃO:  
Option A: Incorrect, as "systemctl" is not used for managing serial port information.  
Option B: Correct, "systemctl" is used to view and control system services.  
Option C: Correct, "systemctl" can start, restart, and stop system services.  
Option D: Correct, "systemctl" can be used to restart and shut down the system.
