RESPOSTA DA QUESTÃO 212
==================================================

ENUNCIADO: How often is the openGauss WDR snapshot executed by default?

ITENS:  
A) 30 minutes  
B) 60 minutes  
C) 120 minutes  
D) 360 minutes

ITEM CORRETO: B

EXPLICAÇÃO: By default, openGauss WDR snapshot data is collected every hour (i.e., snapshot_interval = 1h). The default value of wdr_snapshot_interval is 1h (i.e., 60 minutes). Therefore, we can determine that the answer is 60 minutes, that is B.
