RESPOSTA DA QUESTÃO 103
==================================================

ENUNCIADO: How many standby nodes does openGauss support?

ITENS:  
A) 3  
B) 8  
C) 10  
D) No limit

ITEM CORRETO: B

EXPLICAÇÃO: openGauss supports the HA deployment of one primary node and up to eight standby nodes. In addition, openGauss can be scaled from a standalone or HA system to one primary node with eight standby nodes. This further proves that openGauss supports a maximum of eight standby nodes.
