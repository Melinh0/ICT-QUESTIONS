==================================================

ENUNCIADO: When is openEuler 22.03 LTS planned for end-of-life?

ITENS:  
A) 4596/6  
B) 2027/03  
C) 4570/2  
D) 4601/7  

ITEM CORRETO: B

EXPLICAÇÃO: The openEuler 22.03 LTS version follows the naming convention of openEuler YY.MM, where YY is the year and MM is the month. The LTS version is supported for around 5 years. Since openEuler 22.03 LTS was released in March 2022, its end-of-life is planned for March 2027.
